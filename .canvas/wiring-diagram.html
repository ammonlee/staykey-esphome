A131 is the correct base for AtomS3 Lite.
GPIO5 (RX) / GPIO6 (TX) — confirmed working by the Reddit guide.
K045 (RX:22, TX:19) was designed for the original Atom Lite only. The RS485 traces route to different physical pin positions. If you have the K045, try GPIO8/GPIO7 — or get the A131.
